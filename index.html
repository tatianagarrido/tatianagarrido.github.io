Frua's Website

This was the main project of my Web Design course.
Frua is street food truck with a twist because it sells delicious fruit juices! But the presence online was weak that's why I picked them.
The highlights of this website are the freshness and the modern looks. This bright green is the key for bringing the fresh, healthy, young and ecological vibes. Also picked a sans-serif font for legibility and an all-caps font for headlines for a friendly and modern feel.
This website has a functional data base, where the owners can easily the form's results and update their menu for seasonal fruit juices.

For this website I used: Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Ilustrator, Mockplus (I didn't have access to Sketch), HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, mySQL, Bootstrap and jQuery.
